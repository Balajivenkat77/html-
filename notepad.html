Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions
  • 1/4 cup sliced olives

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Top with pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, and olives.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Remove from oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.

Fruit Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes

Steps:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend on high speed until smooth.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
